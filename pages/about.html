About Me

Hello! I'm Nikolaos, a passionate developer who loves creating meaningful digital experiences that bridge the gap between technology and human connection.

My journey in technology began with a curiosity about how things work and a desire to build solutions that make a difference. I specialize in full-stack development, with particular expertise in modern web technologies and data-driven applications.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or diving deep into the latest developments in machine learning and artificial intelligence. I believe that great software is not just about writing code, but about understanding the people who will use it.

I'm always excited to take on new challenges and collaborate with others who share a passion for creating innovative solutions.

Inspiration from the Masters

The dramatic chiaroscuro and emotional depth of Caravaggio's work deeply influences my approach to creating meaningful digital experiences

The Cardsharps

1594

A masterpiece of psychological drama and deception. This painting teaches me about the importance of understanding user motivations and hidden behaviors in UX design.

David with the Head of Goliath

1609-1610

A powerful study in triumph and melancholy. Like David overcoming Goliath, I believe in tackling complex technical challenges with both skill and humility.

Sacrifice of Isaac

1603

Divine intervention at the crucial moment. This reminds me that the best solutions often come when we're willing to reconsider our approach and remain open to better alternatives.

Crowning with Thorns

1602-1604

Profound suffering rendered with extraordinary empathy. This painting inspires me to create technology that truly understands and alleviates human struggles rather than adding to them.
